Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a mentor

Current students or recent graduates from IIT, AIIMS, or other premier institutions. You need to provide valid college ID and academic credentials for verification.

Earnings vary based on hours worked and your hourly rate (₹500-₹2000/hr). Most mentors earn between ₹30,000 to ₹1,50,000 per month. The average is around ₹85,000/month.

Payments are processed weekly directly to your bank account. You'll receive payment every Monday for sessions completed in the previous week.

No prior teaching experience required! We provide training materials and support to help you become an effective mentor. Your academic credentials and passion for teaching are what matter most.

Completely flexible! You set your own availability and can update it anytime. Work as much or as little as you want - perfect for balancing with your studies or other commitments.

You can teach any subject you're comfortable with - JEE/NEET preparation, college-level courses, competitive exam prep, or specialized topics in your field of study.
